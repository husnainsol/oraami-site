“Do your research” is easy advice and hard practice. In reality, a well-researched lead is a small dossier — assembled before you ever write the first line.

## Signals

Hiring for a role you help with. A fresh funding round. A tooling migration. Timing signals tell you not just who to reach, but when they'll actually care.

## People

B2B deals are won across a committee, not with a single contact. Map the decision maker, the economic buyer, and the champions — and tailor the message to each.

## Proof

The right case study, matched to the prospect's exact context, does more than any clever subject line. Research is what lets you pick the right one.
