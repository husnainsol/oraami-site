The average B2B purchase involves six to ten people. Targeting one of them and hoping they'll champion you internally is how good deals quietly die.

## Map the roles, not just the names

Decision maker, economic buyer, technical evaluator, end user, blocker. Each cares about something different — and each needs a message written for their stake, not a forwarded generic pitch.

## Multithread early

Reaching several stakeholders with tailored, coordinated outreach beats a single thread every time. It's more work by hand — which is exactly why it's worth automating.
