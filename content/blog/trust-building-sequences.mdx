Trust isn't built in one email. The teams that win treat outreach like a relationship — a series of relevant, low-friction touches over weeks, not a single ask.

## Give before you take

Each touch should carry something useful: a relevant insight, a matched proof point, a genuinely helpful question. The meeting is the outcome of value, not the opening demand.

## Patience compounds

Eight to twelve personalised touches over six to twelve weeks feels slow next to a mass blast. But relationships built this way keep paying off — they turn into inbound conversations long after the sequence ends.
