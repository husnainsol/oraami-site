Every rep has felt it: the list is enormous, activity is high, and almost nothing lands. Volume prospecting optimises for sends, not meetings — and the two rarely move together.

## The hidden cost of volume

Blasting thousands of low-fit contacts burns three things at once: your domain reputation, your team's time, and your buyers' patience. Each bad send trains the market to ignore you.

Worse, volume hides the signal. When 98% of your list was never a fit, reply-rate averages tell you nothing about what actually works.

## What 50 changes

Cap each ICP at 50 high-fit accounts and the maths inverts. Reps can research every prospect, map the buying committee, and personalise every message — because the list is small enough to deserve it.

Fewer, better-matched leads don't just feel better. They **reply more, book more, and give you a pipeline you can actually trust.**
